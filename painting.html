Inner art is my usual practice that explores emotions as the pathway to connect with the cosmos. With Chinese ink and brushes, I create on various surfaces: from paper to different scales of canvases.

Cosmic Dialogues

I create in a trance state, as the field of love is flowing through. My painting is shaped by water, as water is the element of emotions – the brush strokes and flow of the ink directly mirroring my dynamic internal experiences.
