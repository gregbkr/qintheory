Charlotte was born and raised in the mountainous Anhui province in China and moved to Toronto, Canada with her family at the age of 15. Throughout her early 20s, she left Canada multiple times to pursue her scientific and artistic education in Switzerland and the UK. Since November 2019, she has been residing in Geneva, Switzerland sprouting her professional life. The dance of art and science in her curiosity and creation is, in a way, the embodiment of her cultural fluidity between the mythical Chinese and the western-scientific way.

Charlotte reading at River Arve in Champel, Geneva, 2018

She received her BSc in Physics from McGill University (2012-2016) and spent one year studying at the University of Geneva as an exchange student and a summer student for the ATLAS Experiment of CERN. Following her study in physics, she solo-traveled in Asia, exploring "other truths” and seeing the voice from her inner world, before moving to London, UK to conduct her two-year master’s. During her study in Innovation Design Engineering (2017-2019) at the Royal College of Art and Imperial College London, she had actively involved herself working in collaboration with scientists and scientific institutes as a designer. These friendships, partnerships include CERN (Geneva, Switzerland), Bell Labs (Cambridge, UK), Campus Biotech (Geneva, Switzerland), University of Edinburgh.
